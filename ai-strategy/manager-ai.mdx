# Manager AI (Ops / Sasha)

**Goal:** Boost creator earnings and reduce support load through intelligent automation.

---

## Overview

Manager AI powers the **Ops (Sasha)** product, acting as a virtual operations manager for creators. It combines wallet signals, integrity data, and behavioral patterns to provide personalized guidance that increases earnings and reduces operational overhead.

## Human-in-the-Loop

### Design Philosophy

<Steps>
  <Step title="AI Analyzes">
    System processes signals and identifies opportunities
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Suggests">
    Generates actionable recommendations with clear reasoning
  </Step>
  <Step title="Human Decides">
    Creator reviews, edits, and approves actions
  </Step>
  <Step title="System Learns">
    Feedback improves future suggestions
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Why This Matters

<Accordion title="Trust & Transparency">
  Creators maintain full control and understand AI reasoning, building trust over time.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Context Awareness">
  Human judgment adds context that AI might miss, improving outcomes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Continuous Improvement">
  User edits and outcomes create a feedback loop for better AI performance.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Regulatory Compliance">
  Human oversight ensures sensitive decisions meet compliance requirements.
</Accordion>
