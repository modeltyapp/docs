# MDLY Holding

**MDLY Holding** is the umbrella for our creator-economy stack:

1. **Modelty (Core):** product direction, platform cohesion, brand & distribution.
2. **Oruon (Flagship, Fintech Wallet):** balances, payout rails, split rules, FX, statements.
3. **RemoveMyContent (Mini-App):** Vault, integrity graph, and enforcement workflows.
4. **Ops (Sasha, Mini-App):** creator operations AI; next-best actions and support automation.

---
