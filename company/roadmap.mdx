# Roadmap & Company Timeline

## Company Evolution

**Origin — Nov 2024 (MVP):** validated that creators prioritize **privacy, protection, and payouts**.

**Scale-up — Mar 2025:** transition to startup; **Oruon** named flagship; mini-apps split responsibilities (Ops & Vault).

**Today — Nov 2025:** wallet-first funnel in place; Ops tuned to wallet/integrity signals; Vault maturing; **AI leak-detection integration in progress**.
