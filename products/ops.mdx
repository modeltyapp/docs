## AI Architecture

### Human-in-the-Loop Design

<Steps>
  <Step title="AI Suggestion">
    System analyzes signals and generates recommendations
  </Step>
  <Step title="Human Review">
    Creator reviews and can edit before action
  </Step>
  <Step title="Action Execution">
    Approved actions are executed with full transparency
  </Step>
  <Step title="Feedback Loop">
    System learns from user edits and outcomes
  </Step>
</Steps>
