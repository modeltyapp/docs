## Product Questions

### Oruon (Wallet)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Oruon a bank?">
    **No.** Oruon is a **creator fintech wallet** that integrates with **licensed partners** for regulated services.

    We provide the interface and functionality, while banking services are delivered through established, licensed financial institutions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What countries is Oruon available in?">
    Currently available in **select jurisdictions** with expansion planned.

    Availability depends on:
    - Partner coverage
    - Regulatory approval
    - Market readiness

    Contact us for specific country availability.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do split rules work?">
    **Split rules** automatically distribute incoming funds according to your configuration:
    - Set percentages or fixed amounts
    - Multiple recipients per rule
    - Time-based rule changes
    - Audit trail maintained

    Example: 70% creator, 20% manager, 10% marketing fund
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the fees?">
    **Free tier:** Basic wallet, standard payouts, essential statements

    **Premium tiers:** Advanced FX, complex splits, enhanced statements, priority support

    Specific pricing available upon request and varies by region.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Ops (Sasha)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does Ops AI work?">
    Ops analyzes signals from:
    - **Wallet:** Earnings trends, payment patterns
    - **Vault:** Protection status, leak detection
    - **Behavior:** Activity patterns, engagement

    It then provides:
    - Next-best action recommendations
    - Smart reply suggestions
    - Personalized playbooks
    - Automated reminders

    All with human-in-the-loop approval.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I turn off AI features?">
    **Yes.** You have granular control over:
    - Which AI features are active
    - What data AI can access
    - When to receive suggestions
    - How much automation to allow

    You can use Modelty with AI features completely disabled if preferred.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Ops read my private messages?">
    **No.** Ops analyzes:
    - Message patterns (not content)
    - Response times
    - Engagement metrics

    Private message content is **never** analyzed unless you **explicitly share** it for smart reply suggestions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does Ops improve earnings?">
    Performance varies by user, but we've documented:
    - RPM/ARPU lift through optimized actions
    - Reduced support load via automation
    - Faster response times

    Specific metrics available under NDA for investors.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Vault (RemoveMyContent)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does Vault protect my content?">
    Vault uses **perceptual hashing** to:
    1. Create unique fingerprints of your original content
    2. Monitor for unauthorized copies across platforms
    3. Generate evidence packs when leaks are detected
    4. Support takedown requests with documentation

    All while maintaining **privacy-first** principles.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Vault use facial recognition?">
    **Only with explicit consent.**

    Default features use:
    - Perceptual hashing (content fingerprinting)
    - Similarity matching
    - Pattern detection

    **No biometric analysis** without clear opt-in.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my content when I upload it?">
    When you upload content:
    1. **Perceptual hash** is generated (fingerprint)
    2. **Original** is stored in your private vault (optional)
    3. **Monitoring** is activated for unauthorized copies
    4. **You retain** full ownership and control

    You can export or delete anytime.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How effective are takedowns?">
    Success depends on:
    - Platform responsiveness
    - Quality of evidence
    - Legal jurisdiction
    - Persistence

    We provide tools and evidence; enforcement is ultimately between you and the platforms.

    Detailed success rates available under NDA for investors.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is my data protected?">
    **Security measures:**
    - Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
    - Encryption at rest
    - Role-based access controls
    - Regular security audits
    - Vendor due diligence

    Full security documentation available under NDA.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who can see my financial data?">
    **Only you** and explicitly authorized parties:
    - You control role-based access
    - Partners you've granted permission
    - Regulatory authorities (when legally required)

    We never sell or share data without consent.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my data?">
    **Yes.** You can export:
    - Complete transaction history (CSV/JSON)
    - Tax-ready statements
    - Media vault content
    - Evidence packs
    - AI interaction history

    Data portability is a core principle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I delete my account?">
    When you delete your account:
    - **Personal data** is removed per GDPR/CCPA
    - **Financial records** retained per legal requirements (typically 7 years)
    - **Content** and hashes deleted immediately
    - **Evidence packs** retained per your request or legal necessity

    Full retention policy disclosed before deletion.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
