# Glossary

Key terms and definitions used throughout Modelty documentation.

---

## A

<Accordion title="ARPU — Average Revenue Per User">
  **Definition:** The average revenue generated per monetized user over a specific time period (typically monthly or annual).

  **Context:** Key metric for SaaS and platform businesses. At Modelty, we report ARPU for monetized users (those paying for premium features or generating transaction revenue).

  **Formula:** Total Revenue ÷ Number of Monetized Users
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Authenticity Certificate">
  **Definition:** A hash-based proof document that verifies original content creation.

  **Context:** Planned feature for Vault that provides cryptographic proof of content ownership, creation time, and integrity.

  **Use cases:** Platform verification, legal proceedings, licensing, NFT metadata.
</Accordion>

---

## B

<Accordion title="Blended Margin">
  **Definition:** The combined profit margin across different revenue streams.

  **Context:** Modelty's blended model (SaaS + Fintech + Platform) yields an attractive blended margin better than pure fintech or pure SaaS alone.
</Accordion>

---

## C

<Accordion title="CAC — Customer Acquisition Cost">
  **Definition:** The total cost of acquiring a new customer, including marketing and sales expenses.

  **Context:** Modelty achieves single-digit cents cost-per-lead, ~6× below LATAM fintech peer averages.

  **Formula:** Total Acquisition Costs ÷ Number of New Customers
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Cohort Analysis">
  **Definition:** Grouping users by acquisition time period to track behavior and retention over time.

  **Context:** Critical for understanding long-term user value and retention patterns. Available under NDA for investors.
</Accordion>

---

## E

<Accordion title="Evidence Pack">
  **Definition:** Comprehensive documentation package for content takedown requests.

  **Context:** Automatically generated by Vault when leaks are detected. Includes original content proof, timestamps, source links, hash comparisons, and ownership verification.
</Accordion>

---

## F

<Accordion title="Flywheel">
  **Definition:** A business model where each component reinforces the others, creating compounding growth.

  **Context:** Modelty's flywheel: Money (Oruon) → Operations (Ops) → Integrity (Vault) → More Money.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="FX — Foreign Exchange">
  **Definition:** Currency conversion services enabling multi-currency transactions.

  **Context:** Oruon provides competitive FX rates through partner networks, enabling creators to hold and convert multiple currencies.
</Accordion>

---

## H

<Accordion title="Human-in-the-Loop">
  **Definition:** AI design pattern where humans review and approve AI suggestions before execution.

  **Context:** Core principle of Ops AI. System suggests actions, creator decides, system learns from feedback.
</Accordion>

---

## I

<Accordion title="Integrity Score">
  **Definition:** Public indicator of a creator's content protection posture in Vault.

  **Context:** Planned dashboard feature showing overall protection status, library coverage, and monitoring health.
</Accordion>

---

## K

<Accordion title="KYB — Know Your Business">
  **Definition:** Identity verification process for business entities.

  **Context:** Required for Oruon accounts representing companies or agencies. Verifies business registration, ownership, and legitimacy.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="KYC — Know Your Customer">
  **Definition:** Identity verification process for individual users.

  **Context:** Required for Oruon wallet accounts to comply with financial regulations. Verifies identity, address, and risk level.
</Accordion>

---

## L

<Accordion title="Lineage Tracking">
  **Definition:** Recording the origin and history of content from original to variants.

  **Context:** Vault maintains complete lineage of protected content, tracking originals, derivatives, and detected copies.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Lock-In">
  **Definition:** Switching costs that make it difficult for customers to move to competitors.

  **Context:** Modelty creates **ethical lock-in** through indispensable utility (money, media proofs, tuned playbooks), not dark patterns.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="LTV — Lifetime Value">
  **Definition:** The total revenue expected from a customer over their entire relationship.

  **Context:** High switching costs and integrated products create high LTV at Modelty. LTV:CAC ratio is key investor metric.

  **Formula:** Average ARPU × Average Customer Lifetime
</Accordion>

---

## M

<Accordion title="MDLY Holding">
  **Definition:** The parent company umbrella for Modelty's creator-economy stack.

  **Structure:**
  - Modelty (Core): Product direction, platform cohesion
  - Oruon (Flagship): Fintech wallet
  - RemoveMyContent (Mini-App): Vault and integrity
  - Ops/Sasha (Mini-App): Operations AI
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Mini-App">
  **Definition:** Focused application that operates within the Modelty ecosystem.

  **Context:** Ops (Sasha) and RemoveMyContent (Vault) are mini-apps that leverage the core Modelty platform and Oruon wallet.
</Accordion>

---

## N

<Accordion title="NDA — Non-Disclosure Agreement">
  **Definition:** Legal agreement protecting confidential information.

  **Context:** Required for access to detailed Modelty metrics, cohort economics, and strategic information.
</Accordion>

---

## O

<Accordion title="Oruón">
  **Definition:** Modelty's flagship creator fintech wallet.

  **Features:** Balances, split rules, statements, payouts, FX, sub-wallets, compliance.

  **Name origin:** Spanish variant of "Orion" (the hunter constellation), representing guidance and navigation.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Ops (Sasha)">
  **Definition:** Modelty's creator operations AI mini-app.

  **Capabilities:** Next-best actions, smart replies, playbooks, reminders, wallet/vault signal integration.

  **Name:** "Sasha" is the internal codename for the AI assistant.
</Accordion>

---

## P

<Accordion title="Perceptual Hash">
  **Definition:** Fingerprint of content that captures visual/audio characteristics rather than exact bytes.

  **Context:** Core technology for Vault. Enables detection of modified content (crops, filters, compression) unlike exact hash matching.

  **Also known as:** pHash, robust hash, fuzzy hash.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Playbook">
  **Definition:** Structured workflow or strategy for achieving specific creator goals.

  **Context:** Ops provides personalized playbooks tied to wallet and integrity signals. Examples: low earnings recovery, expansion strategy, leak response.
</Accordion>

---

## R

<Accordion title="RemoveMyContent (Vault)">
  **Definition:** Modelty's content protection mini-app (private beta).

  **Capabilities:** Perceptual hashing, leak detection, evidence packs, takedown support, integrity scoring.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="RPM — Revenue Per Mille">
  **Definition:** Revenue per thousand impressions or interactions.

  **Context:** Metric commonly used by content creators. Ops AI helps lift RPM through optimized strategies and engagement.
</Accordion>

---

## S

<Accordion title="SaaS — Software as a Service">
  **Definition:** Software licensing and delivery model where applications are hosted centrally and accessed via subscription.

  **Context:** One component of Modelty's blended business model (SaaS + Fintech + Platform).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Split Rules">
  **Definition:** Automated distribution rules for wallet funds across multiple recipients.

  **Context:** Core Oruon feature enabling creators to automatically split earnings with managers, partners, or team members.

  **Example:** 70% creator, 20% manager, 10% production fund.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Sub-Wallet">
  **Definition:** Separate wallet instance within a user's Oruon account.

  **Context:** Enables separation of income streams, business vs. personal funds, project-specific accounting, or team member allocations.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="System of Record">
  **Definition:** The authoritative source for a particular data domain within an organization or ecosystem.

  **Context:** Modelty aims to become the system of record for creator **money** (Oruon), **media lineage** (Vault), and **operations** (Ops).
</Accordion>

---

## T

<Accordion title="TAM — Total Addressable Market">
  **Definition:** The total revenue opportunity available if a product achieved 100% market share.

  **Context:** Modelty's TAM includes all content creators globally. SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market) is more focused subset. Available under NDA.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Takedown">
  **Definition:** The process of requesting removal of unauthorized content from platforms.

  **Context:** Vault provides tools, evidence, and guidance for takedown requests. Success depends on platform policies and legal jurisdiction.
</Accordion>

---

## U

<Accordion title="Unit Economics">
  **Definition:** Revenue and costs associated with an individual unit of business (typically a customer).

  **Context:** Key metrics include CAC, LTV, LTV:CAC ratio, payback period, and contribution margin. Available under NDA for investors.
</Accordion>

---

## V

<Accordion title="Vault">
  **Definition:** Secure storage for original content and perceptual hashes within RemoveMyContent.

  **Context:** Private vault where creators upload originals for fingerprinting, monitoring, and enforcement.
</Accordion>

---

## W

<Accordion title="Wallet-First">
  **Definition:** Go-to-market strategy that prioritizes wallet adoption as the primary entry point.

  **Context:** Modelty's core strategy. Users adopt Oruon for financial value, then expand to Ops and Vault for additional benefits.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="White-Label">
  **Definition:** Product or service produced by one company that other companies rebrand and resell.

  **Context:** Planned offering for agencies and platforms to offer Modelty features under their own brand.
</Accordion>
